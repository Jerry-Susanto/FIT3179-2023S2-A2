Statistics of Top YouTube Channels 2023

This visualisation only cover  YouTube  channels with 12,000,000+ subscribers.

Total  YouTube  Subscribers On Number of Channels of Each Country (2023)

As shown in the choropleth map, the countries with the highest number of  YouTube  subscribers per channel are Cuba, Japan and Venezuela. These three countries are the only countries with over 30,000,000 subscribers per channel. Japan has gotten a recent boost, since PewDiePie, the most popular YouTuber of his generation, has settled there.

The United States of America and India are the two countries with the largest total subscribers, with 7,329,500,000 and 4,310,600,000 respectively. Though, they fall short against the former countries in this case due to their large number of Top YouTube channels.

It is shown that there are only two countries in Africa that has at least one YouTuber with over 12,000,000+ subscribers, Egypt and Morocco. This can be due to the lack of social media users in the Southern parts of Africa.

Subscribers, Views and Uploads Comparison of Top  YouTube  Channels

The plot of subscribers against total views of each  YouTube  channel roughly follows a linear trend. Most of the channels fall on the bottom left, which makes the most popular channels such as T-Series, Cocomelon, SET India, MrBeast and Like Nastya stand out.

The scatter plot on the right shows the increase in subscribers against views of each  YouTube  channel in a recent span of 30 days. The data was recorded 2 months ago, which was around August 2023. Unlike the bubble plot on the left, this plot does not follow a linear trend.

The distribution of  YouTube  subscribers in each continent is compared by the normalised stacked bar chart on the left.

We can see that for Africa, Egypt has the largest distribution of subscribers with around 68%.

In Asia, India has the largest distribution of subscribers with around 58%.

The United Kingdom has the largest distribution of subscribers in Europe with around 38%.

In North America, the United States of America has the largest distribution of subscribers with around 86%.

Australia has the largest distribution of subscribers in Oceania with around 90%.

Finally, Brazil has the largest distribution of subscribers in South America with around 66%.

Lowest & Highest Earnings of the Top 10  YouTube  Channels

In regards to the top 10 channels in the world, both the monthly and yearly earnings follow a similar trend.

T-Series is overall the biggest earner, followed by the kids channel Cocomelon, and SET India. MrBeast, despite being ranked 2nd, falls short in both highest and lowest monthly and yearly earnings.

Number of  YouTube  Channels Per Category                                                          Top 10  YouTube  Channels

Each  YouTube  channel has a category to specify what type of content they upload.

For the top channels in the world, it is shown that Entertainment is the most popular category with 213 channels.

For the top 100 channels, the Music category is the winner with a total of 32 channels.

For the top 10 channels, the Others category which includes Film & Animation, Sport and Show, has the most channels.
